About This Site

This site is a curated, personal archive of traditional and contemporary folk songs. Each track includes details such as the composer, date composed, century, and category — allowing for rich exploration of the music’s history and themes.

Features

  • Audio player for individual or multiple tracks
  • Visualizer that responds to audio playback
  • Hover boxes with song background information
  • Share button for sending playlists via email
  • Planned: Search and filters by composer, date, category, or century

Purpose

Created by a longtime folk music enthusiast, this site serves both as a personal reference and a resource for others interested in the cultural heritage of song. The goal is to make exploration easy, informative, and enjoyable — one folk song at a time.
