Folksong Collection

These are old folk songs, children's songs and ballads recorded with simple guitar and vocals. A line in Yankee Doodle Dandy says "mind the music and the step." For the most part, the melodies are true to the original. The idea is to document folk songs that are in danger of being lost.

The voice and guitar are rough - recorded by an old man on the back porch of a cabin in the woods. Still, if you are wondering how some of these old songs go, this will give you a hint.

Site Navigation

Select songs from any of the playlists shown on the left. Select songs from the playlist and press the "Play" button. This will add selected songs to the playlist in the audio player.

Each time you select new songs from one of the playlists, the previous playlist is automtically cleared from the audio player playlist.

To add songs from more than one playlists to audio player, select them from the "All Tracks" list.

The site should work on smart phones and tablets, but with small fonts and limited audio quality.
