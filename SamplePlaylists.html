Sample Playlists

Curated collections designed to showcase the breadth and beauty of traditional folk music. Each playlist follows a thematic journey with careful attention to flow, tempo, and emotional arc.

Curated Playlists

  • Across the Waters - 9 Items/33 minutes
    Maritime adventures, emigration, and life at sea. From upbeat shanties to haunting farewells, this collection captures the spirit of sailors and emigrants crossing the oceans.
  • Fireside Stories - 8 Items/32 minutes
    Classic ballads and tragic tales, from playful beginnings to emotional catharsis. Perfect for quiet evening listening, these songs tell timeless stories of love and loss.
  • Dust & Rails - 8 Items/33 minutes
    American work songs, railroad ballads, and Depression-era struggles. Woody Guthrie features prominently in this collection celebrating the working class journey.
  • Emerald Threads - Items/30 minutes
    Irish tradition from raucous celebration to patriotic pride, ending with peaceful farewell. A journey through the Emerald Isle's rich musical heritage.
  • Campfire Sing-Along - 9 Items/28 minutes
    Accessible favorites perfect for group singing. From fun to nostalgic to hopeful, these beloved songs invite everyone to join in.
