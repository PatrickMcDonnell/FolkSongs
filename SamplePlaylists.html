Sample Playlists

Curated collections designed to showcase the breadth and beauty of traditional folk music. Each playlist follows a thematic journey with careful attention to flow, tempo, and emotional arc.

Curated Playlists

  • Across the Waters - 9 Items/33 minutes
    Maritime adventures, emigration, and life at sea. From upbeat shanties to haunting farewells, this collection captures the spirit of sailors and emigrants crossing the oceans.
  • Fireside Stories - 8 Items/32 minutes
    Classic ballads and tragic tales, from playful beginnings to emotional catharsis. Perfect for quiet evening listening, these songs tell timeless stories of love and loss.
  • Dust & Rails - 8 Items/33 minutes
    American work songs, railroad ballads, and Depression-era struggles. Woody Guthrie features prominently in this collection celebrating the working class journey.
  • Emerald Threads - 8 Items/30 minutes
    Irish tradition from raucous celebration to patriotic pride, ending with peaceful farewell. A journey through the Emerald Isle's rich musical heritage.
  • Campfire Sing-Along - 9 Items/28 minutes
    Accessible favorites perfect for group singing. From fun to nostalgic to hopeful, these beloved songs invite everyone to join in.

More Curated Collections

  • Murder Ballads - 8 Items/30 minutes
    Dark narratives and cautionary tales from Appalachian tradition. These tragic songs explore themes of betrayal, justice, and consequence.
  • Songs of Conscience - 8 Items/32 minutes
    Anthems of change from the civil rights and anti-war movements. These powerful songs gave voice to movements for justice and peace.
  • Appalachian Roots - 8 Items/28 minutes
    Mountain music: fiddle tunes, spirituals, and old-time ballads. The authentic sound of Appalachia's rich musical heritage.
  • Love & Longing - 8 Items/30 minutes
    Tender ballads of romance, heartbreak, and devotion. These timeless love songs span centuries and continents.
  • Christmas Classics - 10 Items/32 minutes
    Traditional carols spanning centuries, from medieval to Victorian eras. Celebrate the season with these beloved holiday songs.

Journey Through Time

  • Ancient Ballads - 6 Items/24 minutes
    Child Ballads and medieval songs from the British Isles, 16th-18th centuries. The oldest surviving ballads in the folk tradition.
  • Victorian Parlor - 7 Items/26 minutes
    Tin Pan Alley and parlor favorites from the 1850s-1900s. Songs that graced Victorian drawing rooms and music halls.
  • 1960s Revival - 8 Items/30 minutes
    The Greenwich Village scene: Dylan, Seeger, and the protest song era. The folk revival that changed popular music.
